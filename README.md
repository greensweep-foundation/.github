# GreenSweep Foundation

**Turning engagement into verified environmental impact.**

GreenSweep is a Malta Purpose Foundation that directs commercial partnership revenue to environmental projects chosen by community vote. 70% of all revenue flows to projects; the remaining 30% covers operations, verification, and platform development.

## How It Works

1. **Engage** — Participate on the GreenSweep platform (voting, profiling, community activity)
2. **Revenue generated** — Your engagement generates revenue through commercial partnerships
3. **Community votes** — You choose which environmental projects receive funding
4. **Verified impact** — Projects are verified against Gold Standard, Verra, and Plan Vivo standards
5. **Transparent reporting** — Financial allocations will be published and filed with Malta Business Registry

## Our Approach to Transparency

We believe environmental funding should be structurally transparent — not just voluntarily transparent. As a Malta Purpose Foundation (Chapter 16, Civil Code), GreenSweep's mission is legally immutable. It cannot be privatised or redirected.
